Merge two BST 's

Given two BSTs, return elements of merged BSTs in sorted form.

Examples :

Input:
BST1:
       5
     /   \
    3     6
   / \
  2   4  
BST2:
        2
      /   \
     1     3
            \
             7
            /
           6
Output: 1 2 2 3 3 4 5 6 6 7
Explanation: After merging and sorting the two BST we get 1 2 2 3 3 4 5 6 6 7.
